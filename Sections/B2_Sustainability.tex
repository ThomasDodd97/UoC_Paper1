    \begin{large}
        \textbf{Sustainability Spotlight}
    \end{large}
    \begin{justify}
        \begin{normalsize}
            Conventional practices surrounding the use of thermoset plastics
             could be considered at odds with the UN's 12\textsuperscript{th}
             SDG concerning sustainable consumption and production given it's
             dominantly fossil-derived feedstocks and it's most common 
             final-fate of incineration or landfill.
            This work therewith addresses this SDG by providing a elucidating
             the means by which researchers may accelerate their research into
             bio-based alternatives.
        \end{normalsize}
    \end{justify}
\end{center}
